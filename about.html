Our Story

We began with a simple belief: learning is most powerful when it is hands-on, creative, and meaningful. BBIS was founded to create a space where students are encouraged to explore ideas, ask questions, and learn through making. By combining design, technology, and craftsmanship, we give learners the freedom to experiment with materials and tools while developing practical skills. Through real-world projects, collaboration, and reflection, we continue to build a learning environment that fosters curiosity, confidence, and skills that last a lifetime.
